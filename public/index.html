X All The Y

A URL-based meme generator. Arguably the dumbest app I've made yet.

A few examples:

  • The standard one liner: xallthey.com/clean.all.the.things
  • The exotic two liner: xallthey.com/clean.all.the.things/raaaaawr
  • Putting "?/" at the very end engages sad mode: xallthey.com/clean.all.the.things?/

Other modes:

  • Fry: fry.xallthey.com/not.sure.if.trolling/or.just.stupid
  • Good Guy Greg: goodguy.xallthey.com/asked.to.send.error.report/does
  • Scumbag Steve: scumbag.xallthey.com/borrow.your.lighter/permanently
  • Y U NO: yuno.xallthey.com/lady.gaga/y.u.no.turn.off.telephone
  • Dr. Rockso: rockso.xallthey.com/kakakakakaka/yeeeeeaaaah
